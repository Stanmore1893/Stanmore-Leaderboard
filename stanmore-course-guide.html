WHS: Handicap Index → Course Handicap → Playing Handicap
WHS Formula
1
Course Handicap = HI × (Slope ÷ 113) + (Course Rating − Par)
2
Playing Handicap = Round( unrounded Course HCP × Allowance% )
3
Matchplay strokes = Difference in Course Handicaps × Allowance%

⚔️ Matchplay — Stroke Difference
Calculate how many strokes the higher-handicap player or team receives. Supports singles, fourball and foursomes formats.
The allowance % is applied to the difference in Course Handicaps. The higher-handicap player/team receives the resulting strokes. Tick Mixed Tees to add equalisation shots.

Foursomes note: Partners alternate shots — Player A tees odd holes, Player B tees even holes, each from their own tee. The combined team handicap uses both players' Course Handicaps × 50%, and equalisation uses the average Course Rating of both tees.
