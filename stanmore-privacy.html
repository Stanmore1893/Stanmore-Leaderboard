Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Stanmore Golf Club is committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who uses our Digital Hub and email communications. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK GDPR.

1. What we collect

When you sign up to our mailing list, we collect only:

  • Your name — so we can address emails to you personally
  • Your email address — so we can send you the communications you've asked for

We do not collect your date of birth, address, phone number, or any financial information through this mailing list signup.

2. Why we collect it

We use your name and email address only to send you the marketing communications you've opted in to receive, which may include:

  • Exclusive member and visitor offers at Stanmore Golf Club
  • Society and event news
  • Entry into our monthly free fourball prize draw
  • Occasional club news and updates

The legal basis for processing your data is your explicit consent, which you provide by ticking the opt-in box on our signup form.

3. Who we share it with

We use Mailchimp (Intuit Inc.) as our email delivery platform. Your name and email address will be stored on Mailchimp's servers, which may be located outside the UK. Mailchimp is GDPR-compliant and their privacy notice is available at mailchimp.com/legal/privacy.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with any other third party for marketing purposes.

4. How long we keep it

We keep your name and email on our mailing list for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, we remove your details from our active list within 30 days, keeping only a record of your unsubscribe request so we don't accidentally re-add you.

5. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your information ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent — unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of every email we send
  • Complain — lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

6. Data security

Your data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stored securely by Mailchimp. We follow industry-standard practices to protect against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your data.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Substantial changes affecting how we use your data will be communicated by email where possible.

How to contact us

If you have any questions about this policy, or wish to exercise any of your rights, please contact Stanmore Golf Club at enquiries@stanmoregolfclub.co.uk.
