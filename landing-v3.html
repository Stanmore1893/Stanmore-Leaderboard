Features

Built for club golf

Everything you need to run a competition — live scoring, leaderboards, special prizes, handicaps and results — right in your members' pocket.

📱
Score on any phone
Scan a QR code and enter scores hole by hole. Stableford points, nett scores and WHS handicaps calculated live. No app download. Works on any device.
🏆
Live leaderboard
Updates every 5 seconds. Running live as players score. Filter by competition when multiple events run simultaneously. Display in the bar or share the link.
📋
Halfway & final scorecard check
After holes 9 and 18, players see a full scorecard to verify every score before continuing or submitting. Fix any mistake before it's locked in.
🎯
Special prizes
Nearest the Pin, Nearest the Pin in 2 and Longest Drive tracked live. Players claim directly on the hole — a flag appears automatically on the relevant holes.
🧮
Multiple competition formats
Stableford, Strokeplay, Scratch, Par/Bogey and team formats. WHS course rating, slope and allowance applied automatically. Mixed tees with equalisation fully supported.
🔐
Organiser admin panel
Set up competitions, push live announcements, edit scores, manage prize holes and close competitions with a full PDF results sheet — all from your phone.
📶
Offline mode
No signal on the course? Scores save locally and sync automatically the moment connection returns. Nothing is ever lost.
🖨️
Print-ready QR sheet
One-tap A4 QR code sheet covering all five apps — hand it out on competition day so every player can find the right page instantly.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

No installation, no accounts for players, no technical knowledge needed.

1
Admin sets up the competition
Log into the admin panel, add a competition with the format, tee colour and number of holes. One tap to set it as active and it's live immediately.
2
Print the QR code or share the link
The hub generates a QR code for the score entry app. Print it for the first tee, or send the link via WhatsApp the morning of the event.
3
Players score as they play
Open the link on any smartphone — no app download needed. Enter handicap index, select the competition, and tap the score after each hole.
4
Watch the leaderboard update live
The clubhouse iPad shows the live leaderboard refreshing every 15 seconds. Scores appear as players come in from each hole.
5
Export results for prize giving
Download a printable PDF or spreadsheet from the leaderboard — final positions, scores and handicaps ready the moment the last putt drops.
