WHS Handicap Formula Used
Course HCP = HI × (Slope ÷ 113) + (CR − Par)
Playing HCP = Round( unrounded Course HCP × Allowance% )
The stroke allowance is applied to the unrounded Course Handicap before rounding, in accordance with WHS rules. Standard allowance for Stableford and Strokeplay competitions is 95%.
